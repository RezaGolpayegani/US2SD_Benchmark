ALFRED seems to be a personal assistant device or service targeted at older adults. It provides a multitude of services, including managing privacy and data settings, speech and visual interaction, healthcare monitoring, help with physical exercise, reminders, navigation, emergency aid, and digital social interaction. Developers can create and maintain their apps on ALFRED's open platform, and have access to its API, and related development support. The device is to be portable and easily adaptable to user needs as well as being able to learn user preferences.
