This project entails the development of a comprehensive library management system with features tailored to different user roles including the borrower/customer, librarian, library manager, library employee, library member, library visitor, staff, manager, reader, and student. The system will have features such as advanced book search by title, author, genre or publication date, reservation and availability of books, book recommendation system, maintenance of borrower information, updating inventory of books, tracking overdue books or delayed returns, calculation of fines, management of member subscriptions, viewing of book details and library statistics, adding new books and members, updating member profiles and many more. The system will help in increasing efficiency through automation and improving the overall operational workflow of the library.
